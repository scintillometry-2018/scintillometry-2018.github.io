Local Transportation

From Pudong International Airport to the conference venue (Tsung-Dao Lee Institute)
- By taxi: taking around 50 minutes, costing around 180 RMB
- By subway: taking the Subway Line 2 East Extension(direction: Guanglan Road) to the Guanglan Road Station, then taking the Line 2 (direction: East Xujing) to the People’s Square Station, transferring the Line 1 (direction: Xinzhuang) to the Xinzhuang Station, transferring the Line 5 (direction: Minhang Development Zone) to the Dongchuan Road Station. [2 hours 15 minutes, 10RMB] -By maglev and subway: taking maglev at the Pudong International Airport to the Longyang Road [8 minutes, 50RMB]; transferring the Line 2 (direction: East Xujing) to the People’s Square Station, transferring the Line 1 (direction: Xinzhuang) to the Xinzhuang Station, transferring the Line 5 (direction: Minhang Development Zone) to the Dongchuan Road Station. [1 hour 15 minutes, 6 RMB]

From Hongqiao International Airport to the conference venue (Tsung-Dao Lee Institute) 
- By taxi: taking around 40 minutes, costing around 100 RMB
- By subway: taking the Subway Line 10 (direction: Xinjiangwancheng) to the South Shaanxi Road Station first, then taking the Line 1 (direction: Xinzhuang) to the Xinzhuang Station, transferring the Line 5 (direction: Minhang Development Zone) to the Dongchuan Road Station. [1 hours 20 minutes, 6RMB]
